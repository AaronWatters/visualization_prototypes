Magnetic field around a neutron star

This page displays a snapshot of a simulation of a magnetic field surrounding a neutron star. The source data was generated at the Flatiron Institute by Yajie Yuan.

The visualization is interactive. The solid surface represents an isosurface the logarithm of the magnitude of the magnetic field. Adjust the slider to change the isosurface threshold. Rotate the 3d figures by dragging the mouse over the dot cloud.
